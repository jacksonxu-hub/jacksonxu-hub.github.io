Hiroshi Tanaka, our accomplished guitarist and composer, brings a fusion of musical traditions to our band. Born and raised in Tokyo, Japan, Hiroshi's love for music began with his father's collection of jazz records. He immersed himself in various guitar styles, blending elements of jazz, rock, and traditional Japanese music to create a unique sound. Hiroshi's exceptional talent and technical prowess on the guitar have garnered him recognition both in Japan and internationally. When he's not composing or performing, Hiroshi enjoys exploring nature, practicing martial arts, and indulging in his passion for photography. His diverse cultural background enriches the band's artistic expression, creating a harmonious blend of musical influences.

Maya Thompson, a multi-talented instrumentalist, adds depth and melody to our band with her mastery of the keyboard and violin. Hailing from New Orleans, Louisiana, Maya's musical journey started in her family's jazz band, where she honed her skills and developed a unique style blending classical training with jazz and blues influences. Maya's passion for music extends beyond the stage, as she teaches music to underprivileged children in her community. In her spare time, she enjoys painting, practicing yoga, and exploring the vibrant art scene of her city.

Aisha Johnson, a talented vocalist and songwriter, brings soulful melodies and a captivating stage presence to our band. Born and raised in Detroit, Michigan, Aisha's passion for music began at an early age, singing in church and local talent shows. Her powerful voice and heartfelt lyrics resonate with audiences, reflecting her journey of self-discovery and empowerment. Aisha's soulful sound is influenced by her love for R&B, jazz, and gospel music. When she's not performing, Aisha enjoys volunteering at youth music programs and spending time with her family. Her hobbies include reading, cooking, and exploring new coffee shops in the city.
