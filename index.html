Favorite Restaurant and Why I Like It

Go to Restaurant

My favorite restaurant is chipotle because I'm a big fan of Mexican food and chipotle has a pretty generous selection of tortilla, bowls, and tacos with different ingredient options. It's also very affordable and convenient (you can grab and go). Although the meals do have a lot of calories, many ingredients (e.g., beans) are very healthy and you can definitely get a balanced, not overly rich meal if you choose wisely.

Image of Favorite Food and Recipe

I like to serve my steak medium-rare so I usually pan cry it for about 7-8 minutes, flipping it often in the process.
Before cooking, I would marinate the steak with salt and black pepper for about 10-15 minutes. After cooking it for
about 5-6 minutes, I would add butter and peeled garlic to the pan to add some flavour. When the steak is done, I would
take it out of the pan and use the same pan to cook some scrambled eggs and potato chunks to serve with the steak. Image
source: photographed by Tim Toomey, downloaded from https://unsplash.com/s/photos/steak.
